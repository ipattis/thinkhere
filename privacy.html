Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

The short version: ThinkHere runs AI entirely in your browser. Your conversations, documents, and model outputs never leave your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your AI interactions.

1. Who We Are

ThinkHere is a product of Qanata Labs. This policy describes how we handle information in connection with the ThinkHere service at thinkhere.ai and app.thinkhere.ai.

2. Data That Never Leaves Your Device

The core function of ThinkHere — AI inference — happens entirely in your browser using WebGPU. The following is processed and stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

— Conversation messages and AI responses
— Uploaded documents and knowledge base content
— Model files (cached in your browser's Cache API)
— Conversation history (stored in IndexedDB)
— Settings and preferences (stored in localStorage)

We have no technical capability to access any of this data. Clearing your browser's site data permanently deletes it.

3. Account Information (app.thinkhere.ai)

If you create an account at app.thinkhere.ai, we collect and store:

— Your email address
— A hashed password (managed by AWS Cognito — we never see your plaintext password)
— Your subscription status and billing history

This information is used solely to authenticate you and manage your subscription. It is stored securely using AWS Cognito and our backend services.

4. Payment Information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details. Stripe provides us with subscription status and a customer identifier. Stripe's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment information.

5. Analytics and Tracking

ThinkHere does not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics services, or fingerprinting. We do not use cookies for tracking. We may collect basic, anonymised server-level access logs (IP address, page requested, timestamp) for operational purposes only. These logs are retained for a short period and are not used for profiling or advertising.

6. Third-Party Services

ThinkHere uses the following third-party services in the operation of app.thinkhere.ai:

— AWS Cognito — user authentication
— AWS Lambda / API Gateway — subscription management backend
— Stripe — payment processing
— Amazon CloudFront / S3 — static asset delivery

Model weights are downloaded from Hugging Face directly to your browser on first use. Runtime libraries are loaded from jsDelivr. Neither service receives your prompts or model outputs.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and any associated data at any time by emailing contact@qanatalabs.com. Because conversation data lives only on your device, deleting your account deletes the only data we hold — your email, authentication credentials, and subscription record.

8. Children

ThinkHere is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. We will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated where possible.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests? Email us at contact@qanatalabs.com.
